About
Developer Student Club Taft is the first ever student organization under Google Developers and part of the first batch of chapters established in the Philippines. We are a community of developers that are passionate about uplifting communities through technology and innovation. We aim to help students bridge the gap between theories and practice. By joining a DSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.
